Tomato Egg Rice Stir Fry

General Description and History

Also known as 家常菜 (jia chang cai). Too many Chinese people, this dish represents home-cooked comfort food. Most families have their own versions. Its a simple and quick meal to whip up in just 10 minutes meanwhile being cheap.

Ingredients

  • 4 - 10 tomatoes
  • 1 scallion
  • 4 eggs
  • 3/4 tsp salt
  • 1/4 tsp white pepper
  • 1 tsp shaoxing wine
  • 3 tbsp vegetable oil
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1-4/1-2 cup water

Steps

  1. Start by cutting tomatoes into small wedges and finely chop the scallion.
  2. Crack 4 eggs into a bowl and season with ¼ teaspoon salt, ¼ teaspoon white pepper, ½ teaspoon sesame oil, and 1 teaspoon Shaoxing wine. Beat eggs for a minute.
  3. Preheat the wok over medium heat until it just starts to smoke. Then add 2 tablespoons of oil and immediately add the eggs.
  4. Scramble the eggs and remove from the wok immediately. Set aside.
  5. Add 1 more tablespoon oil to the wok, turn up the heat to high, and add the tomatoes and scallions.
  6. Stir-fry for 1 minute, and then add 2 teaspoons sugar, ½ teaspoon salt, and ¼ cup water (if your stove gets very hot and liquid tends to cook off very quickly in your wok, add a little more water). Add the cooked eggs.
  7. Mix everything together, cover the wok, and cook for 1-2 minutes, until the tomatoes are completely softened.
  8. Uncover, and continue to stir-fry over high heat until the sauce thickens to your liking. Serve!

Enjoy!
